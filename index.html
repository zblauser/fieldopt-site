Built for Real Dispatch

Not another startup dashboard. An actual dispatch console designed by someone who works in the field.

⚙

Intelligent Routing

Auto-route evaluates skill, route zone, shift time, and distance to find the best tech for every job. Override warnings keep dispatchers in control.

⚖

Management Areas

Techs are assigned geographic zones. Jobs carry route criteria. The system knows when a job is out of zone and warns before assignment.

★

Drag & Drop Dispatch

Drag jobs onto techs. Multi-select with Cmd+click. Batch assign, batch unassign. Right-click context menus with skill-filtered tech lists.

📈

Tech Timeline

Visual hour-block timeline shows each tech's day at a glance. Overlapping jobs stack. Gaps are visible. Select multiple techs to compare.

🔍

Job Search & Filters

Search historical, current, and future jobs by any criteria. Display filters slice by time slot, job type, route, and tech. Drag results into the grid.

🌎

Multi-Monitor Map

Map detaches to its own window for multi-monitor dispatch setups. Live data sync keeps markers current as assignments change.
